About OG Properties

Your trusted partner in student housing

Our Story

OG Properties was founded with a simple mission: to provide quality, affordable housing for students while offering property owners a hassle-free management experience.

We understand the unique challenges of student housing - from the need for flexible lease terms to maintaining properties that feel like home. That's why we've built a platform that serves both students and property owners with equal dedication.

Today, we manage properties near campus, providing safe, comfortable, and convenient living spaces for students while maximizing returns for property owners.
