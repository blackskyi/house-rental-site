How to Find Coordinates

  1. Go to Google Maps
  2. Find your property and right-click on the exact location
  3. Click "What's here?" or the coordinates at the bottom
  4. Copy the coordinates (first number = latitude, second = longitude)
  5. Paste them into the form below and click "Save & Preview"
